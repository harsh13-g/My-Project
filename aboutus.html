__SINCE 1998__

Almost 9 decades ago, ''Suleman sha'' the
industrious man behind the brand, laid the
foundation of a small shop in Gariyadhar - 'The
City of kings'. In 1998, a separate identity
was given to this journey by The King as ''M S''.

A box of our mithai is quite more than a sweet symphony for your tastebuds. It brings back smiles, fond memories, and sticky fingers that require immediate attention. Our sweets and savories are made day after day to confirm the great taste and also the highest level of freshness.

We have been dedicated for decades to delivering the supreme level of taste to you and we are promising to be constant. MS has become the legitimate name in top-quality sweets and savories as we believe that taste should not be compromised.

MS have revived artisanal mithai-making by respecting traditions, the history behind every recipe, and the way it had been created. Every choice is fastidiously organized and fantastically packaged to make an exquisite box of mithai. Each flavor that we tend to unleash has its distinctive journey that makes every bite emotive. Our most popular sweets include Boondi Laddu, Gol Pattisa, Kalajam, Milk Pudding, and Mewa Bites.
